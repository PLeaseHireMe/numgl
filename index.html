A simple example of a convolution kernel (3x3 matrix in this case) applied to video (webcam).
Note that the kernel is being applied to every single pixel, on all the 3 channels (red, green and blue).
That's 640x480 pixels, 3 channels, multiplied by a 3x3 matrix, and I'm getting 50 FPS on my old budget laptop. Insane!
